OUR STORY

Building Nigeria's Future, One Project at a Time

Wilson Caterpillar Nigeria Enterprises (Wilsoncat) was founded with a singular vision: to provide Nigerian contractors, construction companies, and industrial operators with access to genuine Caterpillar parts and reliable heavy equipment.

For over 15 years, we've been at the forefront of Nigeria's infrastructure development, supplying the parts and machinery that power the nation's most ambitious construction and mining projects.

From the bustling markets of Abuja to remote construction sites across the country, Wilsoncat has become synonymous with quality, reliability, and professional service in the heavy equipment industry.
